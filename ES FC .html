Spanish Football

One of the best teams in the world!

Spain is one of eight national teams to have been crowned world champions and has participated in a total of 16 out of 22 FIFA World Cups, winning the 2010 edition, and qualifying consistently since 1978. Spain has participated in a total of twelve out of seventeen UEFA European Championships, winning their first in 1964, and are the current European champions, having won a record fourth title in 2024.
